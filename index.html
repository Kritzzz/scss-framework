Does this line-height make me look fat?

The beginning of this text is important. Paragraphs have a font-size of 1em - in thise case 16px - and a resulting line-height of 26px (1.625 * 16px), which gives the lines breathing space and makes the text easier to read.

Each paragraph also has a bottom margin of the same 26px used for the line-height. However, not all paragraphs have to look the same. This part is emphasized because it's probably important! Paragraphs might have inline links. Amet quibusdam quasi pariatur? Repudiandae tempora impedit reiciendis ex?
